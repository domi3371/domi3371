Amusingly Amusing

by Esthela Dominguez

This page's sole purpose is to share some websites that I've found funny and entertaining.

Is DMX In Jail

Have you been wondering if DMX, the famous rapper, is currently in jail? This site only features a picture of the rapper as the background and either "DMX is not in jail" or "DMX is in jail". It's a fairly straightforward website.

isdmxinjail

Geoguessr

Geoguessr is a fun game that utilizes google maps to drop you off anywhere on Earth. You can either explore at your leisure or compete to find locations as challenge rounds. It's a great way to pass the time and see the world from the comfort of your couch.

geoguessr

Presidential Ham

This site depicts artistic renderings of all the United States President with ham in their arms. It's odd, but entertaining and slightly charming.

presidentialham
