Featured

Hokkaido Flower Fields:

Rolling fields of flowers are one of Hokkaido's stample must-see views. Get an upclose view of 4 of Hokkaido's most beautiful flower fields: Farm Tomita, Shikisai Hill, Kamiyubetsu Tulip Park, and Higashi Mokoto Shibazakura Park. All flower fields specialize in a specific flower such as Moss Phlox, Lavender, and Tulpis.Enjoy seasonal festivals with plenty of food, tractor rides, and go-kart racing.

Sushi Miyakawa:

Enjoy Chef Miyakawa's finest dishes in a newly-awarded Michelin 3 star restaurant. Located in Sapporo, Hokkaido, the exclusive restaurant specializes in top-grade sushi using the freshest seafood in Japan. Be one of 16 lucky people who enjoy internationally reknowned meals per day.

Noboribetsu Hot Springs:

Southwest Hokkaido, Noboribetsu is one of the world's most popular hot spring towns. Offering nine different qualities of mineral-rich water, Noboribetsu is the relaxation spot in Japan. The hot springs are surrounded by beautiful dense forest and setled in a small, but welcoming town.
