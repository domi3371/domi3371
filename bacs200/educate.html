TED Talks

Amy Cuddy - Your Body Language Shapes Who You Are

  • Is it possible to fake it until you make it? Amy Cuddy speaks on how our body language may not just be a reflection of our minds, but also a beginning to new self confidence. We have the ability to shape ourselves into better versions of ourselves with just a pose.

    • Lucinda Beaman - What Does It Take To Change A Mind?

  • Changing our minds is no easy matter. We'd need to vulnerable, learn, and truly understand why we might be wrong on a matter in order to even begin the process of changing our minds. However, becoming more open minded can lead us to a better, healthier society.

    Smokey Jungle Frog

  • Off the coast of Costa Rica, the smokey jungle frog are famous for their incredible loud and distinct yell. Coyote Peterson takes it upon himself to search the dense forest in search of one of these frogs to give us the opportunity to see them up close.

    • Tarancula Hawk

  • The tarancula hawk has the second most painful sting in the insect kingdom; second only to the bullet ant. Here, Coyote Peterson decides to experince first hand what it's like to be stung by the tarancula hawk.
